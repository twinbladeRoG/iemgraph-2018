IEMGRAPH 2018

The first International Conference on Emerging Technology in Modeling and Graphics (IEMGraph-2018) will be held at IEM Gurukul Campus, Kolkata during September 6th-7th, 2018. The IEMGraph’18 conference is a forum for promoting interaction among researchers in the field of India and abroad. The conference will include keynote talks, regular papers and short communications. IEMGraph’18 also offers pre-conference workshops where the researchers can exchange their knowledge with other countries.


Papers are invited on substantial, original and unpublished research on all aspects of the below-mentioned sub-themes. The works within the scope are acceptable for the presentation in the conference. The areas of interest include, but are not limited to:


  • Image processing and analysis
  • Image segmentation
  • Digital geometry for computer imaging
  • Image and Security
  • Biometrics
  • Video processing and object tracking, gait and gesture recognition
  • Medical imaging
  • Computational geometry
  • Image Processing using Deep Learning
  • Virtual and augmented Reality

Publications

The proceedings of IEMGraph’18 will appear in the Springer’s Advances in Intelligent Systems and Computing series.
