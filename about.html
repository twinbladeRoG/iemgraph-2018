The 1st International conference IEMGraph’18 – International Conference on Emerging Technology in Modelling and Graphics, will be held 6-7 th September 2018 in Kolkata, India. The conference will include keynote talks, regular papers and poster communications. IEMGraph’18 seeks outstanding papers describing contributions to the theoretical and practical aspects of image processing and graphical modeling of objects presented in images along with applications in this field.


The proceedings of IEMGraph’18 will be included in the Springer’s Advances in Intelligent Systems and Computing series (AISC).


IEMGraph’18 also offers pre-conference workshops where the researchers can exchange their knowledge with other countries of common interests. We may also organize a Skype session for the presenter who is unable to attend our conference for a proper reason with prior permission. The conference offers a poster presentation session to demonstrate a good quality research work in this arena throughout the conference. Also, registration fees can be paid through credit card or bank transfer. Accommodation may be given but in that case, accommodation fees should be included in registration fees.


Topics
  • Image processing and analysis
  • Image segmentation
  • Digital geometry for computer imaging
  • Image and Security
  • Biometrics
  • Video processing and object tracking, gait and gesture recognition
  • Medical imaging
  • Computational geometry
  • Image Processing using Deep Learning
  • Virtual and augmented Reality

Publications
